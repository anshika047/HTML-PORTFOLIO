The Best Movies According to Anshika

My top 5 movies of all time.

3 Idiots

A heartwarming story about friendship, dreams, and choosing passion over pressure.

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

A beautiful journey that reminds us to step out of our comfort zones and truly enjoy life.

The Pursuit of Happyness

An inspiring true story about resilience, hope, and never giving up.

Interstellar

A breathtaking sci-fi adventure that explores love, sacrifice, and the mysteries of the universe.

The Shawshank Redemption

A timeless story showing that hope and perseverance can overcome even the darkest times.
